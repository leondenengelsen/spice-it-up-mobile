Welcome to Spice It Up!

What is Spice It Up?

Spice It Up is your personal AI-powered recipe assistant that helps you discover exciting new dishes based on your preferences, dietary restrictions, and available ingredients.

How to Use

  1. Enter your ingredients or describe what you're craving
  2. Adjust your portions and adventurousness level in Settings
  3. Set your dietary restrictions and allergies
  4. Get personalized recipe suggestions from our AI chef
  5. Save your favorite recipes to your collection

Features

  • AI-powered recipe generation
  • Dietary restriction support (vegetarian, low FODMAP, etc.)
  • Allergy-friendly suggestions
  • Portion size customization
  • Adventure level adjustment
  • Favorite recipe collection

Tips for Best Results

Be specific about your ingredients and preferences. The more details you provide, the better your recipe suggestions will be. Don't be afraid to experiment with different adventure levels!
